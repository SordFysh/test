About SkyCase.

The SkyCase was born out of a desire for a case that is simple, durable and aesthetically pleasing. The objective was to create a minimalist case that used few parts and every design element had a purpose.

Our Product

SkyCase

The SkyCase vents are formed by our “Wing” logo. The vents also allow the camera ribbon cable to be fed through it, eliminating the need for a dedicated slot for the camera cable. Instead of having a long screw that connects to nuts at the top of the case, the top and bottom are screwed down into standoffs or connector nuts. That means both the top and bottom have a clean, finished look.


Something Provocative

Features

  • A single clear ¼ inch acrylic front and back.
  • Camera cable can exit the case through the logo.
  • The GPIO cable perfectly fits in the opening on the side.
  • A finished look on both sides of the case.
  • Open design allows plenty of airflow through the case.
